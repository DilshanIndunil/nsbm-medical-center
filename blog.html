Our Services For 2022

  1. Conducting of medical examinations for new entrants

  2. Administering of vaccinations on a needs basis

  3. Medical counseling

  4. Laboratory testing

  5. Maintenance of the university premises including the halls of residence

  6. Examination of all personnel handling food items



In addition to these services, University Health Centers issue medical certificates to staff and students upon request, recommend special medical leave for students when necessary.


Facilitate specialist advice and inpatient treatment on referral to the Sri Lanka National Hospital, conduct special medical procedures.


Conducting scholarship examinations for staff, general medical examination of all new recruits and providing medicines to university staff and students for the purpose of prolonging the service of staff.
